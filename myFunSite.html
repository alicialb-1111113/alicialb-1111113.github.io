Watch Hulu

Hulu has a wide variety of movies, tv shows, and documentaries. Have a laugh with a comedy, get scared with a horror, or fiesty with some drama. Whatever your favorite genre is, Hulu has it and you can enjoy it all from the comfort of your own home (or anywhere that has internet on most devices).


Cost: $5.99 per month (1st month free). There are also bundle options like Hulu, Disney+, and ESPN+ for only $12.99 per month!

Make a Delicious Meal

Want to make a scrumptious meal in only 30 minutes?! This site gives you access to 84 different recipes of great meals that only take 30 minutes to make. You can do this anywhere there is a kitchen to cook in. Whether it's just for you or for a dinner party with friends this is always a great option!


Cost: $30-$60 (amount may vary based on number of people you are cooking for)

Solve Puzzels for Science on FoldIt

Through folding proteins in puzzels, you could possibly help prevent or cure diseases. You can do this anywhere you have internet access. If you've ever wanted to contribute to science, this is a great fun way to do it!


Cost: FREE!
